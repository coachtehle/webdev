Privacy Policy

This is a draft policy provided as a starting point. Please have it reviewed by qualified legal counsel before relying on it as your operating policy.

Who we are

The Liminal Leadership Institute is a systemic leadership consultancy. This policy describes how information is handled when you visit this website or use the contact form.

Information we collect

When you submit the contact form, we collect the name, email address, and message you provide. We do not currently use cookies, analytics scripts, or third-party trackers on this site.

How we use it

Information submitted through the contact form is used only to respond to your inquiry and to keep a record of the correspondence. We do not sell, rent, or share this information with third parties.

Retention

We keep contact form submissions only as long as needed to respond to your inquiry and maintain reasonable business records, after which they are deleted.

Your choices

You can ask us what information we hold about you, request a correction, or request deletion at any time by reaching out through the contact form on the homepage.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date above.
